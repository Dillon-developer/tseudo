What is Tseudo?


Tseudo is an exploration game. Play as someone whom is lost in the shadows of a mysterious land.

Find your way out of the darkness in this arcade style procedurally generated platformer.

Collect insight to increase your perception of reality!

Avoid strange entities that will lead you to your demise.

Climb the ranks and test your speed! Find yourself on a global Leaderboard when you finally find the way!
